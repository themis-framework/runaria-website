Privacy Policy  ·  Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Introduction

Cadens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Guest Mode

When using Cadens in Guest Mode, we do not collect any personal information. Your training data is stored locally on your device only.

Account Mode

When you create an account with Cadens, we collect:

  • User ID: A unique identifier automatically generated for your account
  • Training Data: Questions, answers, and training progress you create within the app

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Names or surnames
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of the app
  • Store your training questions, answers, and progress
  • Enable optional coach collaboration features

Coach Collaboration Feature

Cadens offers an optional feature that allows you to work with coaches:

  • You may voluntarily share your User ID with a coach
  • Once shared, the coach can access your training progress, questions, and answers
  • This sharing is entirely opt-in and under your control
  • You are responsible for deciding whether to share your User ID with a coach
Important: We do not facilitate or verify coach relationships. Sharing your User ID is at your own discretion.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your training data (questions and answers) is stored on our secure servers
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • However, we cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted over the internet
User Responsibility: We strongly discourage storing sensitive personal information, medical details, or confidential data in your training questions and answers. While our app is designed for memory training, you should avoid including information that could harm you if accessed by others.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With coaches you authorize: When you explicitly share your User ID with a coach
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Use the app in Guest Mode without creating an account
  • Access your training data at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Choose whether to share your User ID with coaches

Children's Privacy

Cadens does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of Cadens after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you request deletion
  • Guest mode data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time

International Users

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using Cadens, you consent to such transfers.

Contact Us

Email: themis-framework@proton.me

Third-Party Services

Cadens may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Analytics (anonymized usage statistics only)
  • Push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the categories of personal information we collect and how we use it. Since we do not collect traditional personal information (email, name, etc.), this right has limited applicability to our service.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the service
  • Data Subject Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Your Consent
